Brand Identity · Event Production · Campaign Art Direction

BCA Dance Competitions

Ongoing freelance, 5 years, 5–7 events annually

BCA runs competitive dance events across multiple disciplines, contemporary, K-pop, street dance, and championship formats. For five years I've been their sole designer, owning the full visual identity and physical production for every event: 5 to 7 competitions a year, each built out from an established sub-brand through to the printed backdrop.

1 / 10

The Brief

Every event needed its own identity, different discipline, different audience energy, different feel, with no repeated visual language between them. And someone had to own all of it: not just the campaign graphics, but the physical objects people touch and stand in front of on competition day.

What I Own

For each competition: the full poster and digital campaign, targeted paid ads, and every physical asset on-site, backdrops, awards, and medals, designed flat in Illustrator with the assembled object in mind, working directly with the production vendor on shape and finish within budget. I also provide ongoing support and maintenance for the BCA website.

AI in this project

AI-assisted image generation and compositing support the visual production for select campaigns, backgrounds, effects, and imagery integrated with photography and graphic elements, letting each event's identity come together fast within a tight annual cycle.

Outcome

Five years of continuous, independent ownership: every logo, every campaign, every physical asset, across 25–35+ individual events to date, with no drop in quality or consistency despite a new brand identity required every time.
